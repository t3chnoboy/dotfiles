Sometime's things just don't go as planned

Help! Photo Zoom for Facebook stopped working!

PZ4FB's icon will tell you its status in the lower right of the page next to your chat status.

First, make sure that the extension is enabled and that the extension is on from your Chrome Extensions page. When PZ4FB is active you should see an icon in the lower right by your chat status, and when you hover it should say "Photo Zoom for Facebook (on)" (see screenshot). If it says "(off)" simply click the icon to turn PZ4FB back on.

That icon is missing!

If the extension is correctly enabled on your Chrome Extensions page, then in nearly all cases a hard refresh (hold shift and press refresh) will fix PZ4FB. If you still aren't seeing the icon-box near the chat and you're sure the extension is enabled correctly then something may have gone wrong. Feel free to contact me via my website with as much detail as you can regarding the issue (browser version, operating system, Facebook nationality, etc).

If PZ4FB seems broken, 99% of the time it can be fixed by refreshing the page.

Occasionally Facebook might release an update which may break the extension. There is usually a fast turn around if this is the case.
